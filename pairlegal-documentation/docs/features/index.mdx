# Features

SingleDraft offers a range of powerful features to enhance your legal document workflow:

- [AI Assistant](AI.mdx) - Get agentic assistance with your legal documents
- [Snippets](snippets.mdx) - Search and insert clauses from your internal data
- [Definition Tracking](definition-extraction.mdx) - Track and manage defined terms in
  your documents
- [Checks](checks.mdx) - Validate your documents for common formal issues
- [External Information](external_information.mdx) - Connect your documents with
  external data sources
