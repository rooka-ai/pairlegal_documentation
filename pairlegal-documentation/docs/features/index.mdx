# Features

SingleDraft offers a range of powerful features to enhance your legal document workflow:

- [AI Assistant](/docs/features/AI) - Get agentic assistance with your legal documents
- [Snippets](/docs/features/snippets) - Search and insert clauses from your internal data
- [Definition Tracking](/docs/features/definition-extraction) - Track and manage defined terms in
  your documents
- [Checks](/docs/features/checks) - Validate your documents for common formal issues
- [External Information](/docs/features/external_information) - Connect your documents with
  external data sources
