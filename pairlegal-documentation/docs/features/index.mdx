# Features

SingleDraft offers a range of powerful features to enhance your legal document workflow:

- [Definition Tracking](definition-extraction.mdx) - Track and manage defined terms in your documents
- [Alerts](alerts.mdx) - Get notified about important issues in your documents
- [External Information](external_information.mdx) - Connect your documents with external data sources
- [AI Assistant](AI.mdx) - Get intelligent assistance with your legal documents
- [Editing](editing.mdx) - Tools and features for efficient document editing
