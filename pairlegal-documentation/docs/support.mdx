# Support

We are here to help with any questions or issues you may have with SingleDraft.

## Contact Us

### Email

For questions, feedback, or support requests, please email us at:
**[info@singledraft.ai](mailto:info@singledraft.ai)**

### Intercom Chat

You can also contact us directly from the SingleDraft plugin:

1. Look for the chat icon in the bottom-right corner
2. Click on the icon to open the chat window
3. Type your message and send it

## Report a Bug

When reporting a bug, ideally please include:

- A description of what happened
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- File that caused the issue
- Your operating system information (Windows, macOS, etc.) and Word version

This helps us fix issues faster.

## Request a Feature, Share Feedback

Your feedback helps make SingleDraft better. We welcome all comments and suggestions
about your experience.
